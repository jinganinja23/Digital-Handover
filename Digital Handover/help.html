How To Use

Window 1:

  1. Enter Bartender name
  2. Select the day of the week that tomorrow is
  3. Click next

How much we have Window:

This window is for you to enter how much we have of each item

  1. Enter info either using the text-boxes or increase and decrease buttons
  2. Click Confirm

How much to prep Window:

This window looks just like the last page, but has a different purpose.
It takes the data you entered on the last page and compares it to the
amount needed for the next day, then shows you these amounts in the
same little text-boxes, for you to change if needed.

  1. Change the amount to prep if needed
  2. Click Confirm

Notes Window:

  1. add any notes or inspirational quotes if needed
  2. Click Confirm

Handover Window:

This window displays all the entered info unless no prep is needed for the item, scroll up and down on the window
to fit as much information on the screen, take as many screenshots needed and send to the groupchat
